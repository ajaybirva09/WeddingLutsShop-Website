Frequently Asked Questions

What software are your products compatible with? +

LUTs: Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro X (via plugins), Filmora, LumaFusion
Transitions: Premiere Pro (.mogrt), After Effects (.aep), Final Cut Pro (.fcpx)
Templates: Premiere Pro (.prproj), After Effects (.aep)

How do I download the products after purchase? +

Immediately after successful payment, you'll see a download section with direct ZIP file links. You'll also receive an email with download links. Each purchase comes with a ZIP file containing all files and detailed installation instructions.

Are these suitable for LOG footage? +

Yes! Our LUT packs include specific utility LUTs for S-Log, C-Log, and V-Log footage. We provide LOG to Rec.709 conversion LUTs that ensure the final cinematic LUT is applied correctly.

What is included in the ZIP files? +

LUT Packs: .CUBE files, PDF guide, sample footage
Transition Packs: .mogrt files, .aep files, tutorial videos
Template Packs: Project files, placeholder media, font files, tutorials
All packs include a commercial license and lifetime updates.

What is your refund policy? +

Due to the digital nature of our products, all sales are final and non-refundable. However, we offer free samples so you can test quality before purchasing. If you have any issues with your purchase, our support team will help you resolve them.
