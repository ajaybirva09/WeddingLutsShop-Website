Frequently Asked Questions

What software are your LUTs compatible with? +

Our LUTs are industry-standard .cube files, compatible with Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro X (via plugins), LumaFusion, and most other video editing software that supports 3D LUTs.

How do I download the LUTs after purchase? +

Immediately after successful payment, you'll see a download section with direct ZIP file links. You'll also receive an email with download links. Each purchase comes with a ZIP file containing all .CUBE files and installation instructions.

Are these suitable for LOG footage (S-Log, C-Log, V-Log)? +

Yes. Most of our packs include specific utility LUTs designed to correctly convert LOG footage to Rec. 709, ensuring the final cinematic LUT is applied correctly for the best results.

What is included in the ZIP file? +

Each ZIP file contains: All .CUBE LUT files, PDF installation guide, PDF color grading tips, sample footage files for testing, and a license.txt file.

What is your refund policy? +

Due to the digital nature of our products, all sales are final and non-refundable. Please ensure you have the correct software before purchase. Our support team is happy to answer any questions beforehand.
